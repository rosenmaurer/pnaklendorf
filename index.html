ABOUT ME

My name is Ricardo Meyer . I'm a writer from Chile devoted to cosmic horror and weird fiction.

Since 2024, I have been co-directing the Refugio Bizarro collective, managing its eZine, and working on projects related to the Cthulhu Mythos within the Hispanic weird fiction scene.

I have edited and administered the Hispanic Lovecraft Wiki since 2010, contributing to the preservation and expansion of Cthulhu Mythos in Spanish.

You can find me on Twitter as @PaterDagon .
