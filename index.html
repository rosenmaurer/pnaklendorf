ABOUT ME

My name is Ricardo Meyer . I'm a writer from Chile devoted to cosmic horror, weird fiction, and metaphysical narratives.

Since 2024, I have been co-directing the Refugio Bizarro collective, managing its eZine, and working on projects related to the Cthulhu Mythos within the Hispanic literary scene.

You can find me on Twitter as @PaterDagon .
